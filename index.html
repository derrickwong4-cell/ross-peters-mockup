The Methodology

THE BODY
as one
SYSTEM

Functional Patterns is a biomechanics-based training system founded by Naudi Aguilar. Instead of treating where it hurts, FP traces pain back to the mechanical fault causing it. We study how the human body moves at its best, then correct what's off.

01

ASSESS YOUR STRUCTURE

We analyze your posture, gait, and movement patterns to identify the dysfunctions causing pain, restriction, or imbalance.

02

CORRECTIVE TRAINING

FP retrains your body through four fundamental human movements: standing, walking, running, and throwing. Everything else is built on top of these.

03

LASTING RESULTS

Clients walk out of here standing taller, moving easier, and hurting less. These aren't band-aid fixes. They're structural corrections that hold up months and years later.
